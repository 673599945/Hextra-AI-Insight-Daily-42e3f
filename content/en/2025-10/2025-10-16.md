### Cutting-Edge Research

1.  **Tencent AI Lab has dropped a bombshell with its new Training-Free Group-Relative Policy Optimization (GRPO) technology.** This tech managed to achieve the same results as traditional fine-tuning costing 70,000 RMB, but for a mere 120 RMB (‚úß‚àÄ‚úß)! 2.  **Can code generated by large models just "run and that's it"? A new study suggests these models are often "fair-weather friends," crashing when faced with invalid input because they don't understand how to adhere to a **code contract** (Contract) üßê.** To address this, researchers introduced the **PACT framework**, acting like a strict "code quality inspector."
